Golden Circle Theory

Golden Theory is a concept that challenges people and organizations to think, act, and communicate from the inside out, starting from the WHY to WHAT

Instead of talking about just what I do, I want to focus and talk about WHY I'm doing what I love

The WHY is my mission, my belief, my aspirations

The HOW is the strategy I'm taking, the plan I'm coming up with, the network I'm developing to answer the WHY

The WHAT is the tangibles of my life (job position, skill sets, personality, portfolio)
